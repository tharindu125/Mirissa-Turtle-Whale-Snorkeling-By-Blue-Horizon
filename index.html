About Us

At Mirissa Turtle Snorkeling & Fishing Club, we create unforgettable adventures where the ocean comes alive. Whether swimming with sea turtles, exploring vibrant reefs, or spotting whales, every tour is designed with your safety and joy in mind.

With professional guides, modern equipment, and years of experience, we ensure you’ll enjoy the best snorkeling, diving, and fishing experiences Mirissa has to offer.
